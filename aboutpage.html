Overview

A real-time, online checklist program, mBird has revolutionized the way that the birding community reports and accesses information about birds. Launched in 2017 by the Information Technology lab of Velammal College of Engineering and Technology. mBird provides rich data sources for basic information on bird abundance and distribution at a variety of spatial and temporal scales. mBird goal is to maximize the utility and accessibility of the vast numbers of bird observations made each year by recreational and professional bird watchers. It is amassing one of the largest and fastest growing biodiversity data resources in existence. In time these data will become the foundation for a better understanding of bird distribution across different areas.

For Beginners

So that your account is enable and logged out. You have to sign up with your username and password in our homepage. After you login you would be directed to My Profile page. Now you can select the Bird name, Location of the bird migration ie, state and city from the drop down list. To ensure the correct location type the area(address) in the text area. Enter the total count of the bird and submit it.If you are in record with more than one bird then use the Add Bird button and finally submit it once all the entries are filled. Finally logout your profile without fail.
